Purpose Audience Design

Audience

People who use mobile, PC and any device. Tourists, product buyers, relocation, food lovers, historians and many more. Business owners and such.

Personas

Persona 1: Sophia Lakandula: Sophia is a 43-year-old house wife and is self-employed in her business of selling baked yema cakes. She's happily married and has 2 children. She uses her phone daily to promote her product and ordering online. She uses messenger, facebook, gmail, call and text in communication. One of her goals is to have a very suitable house environment for her family and has been looking for new place to relocate. She surfs the internet looking for homes and wants to be aware of prices, environment and quality of that home.

Persona 2: Miguel Santos: Miguel is 29-year-old professional photographer, lives alone and is connected to the internet all day. He has lot of devices accessing the internet such as laptop, mobile, iPad and a Personal Computer. His job includes capturing scenes of events, sights, occasions, food, and trends. Now he's looking for upcoming events and occasions that he can go to and wants to know the date and time of the event.

Scenarios

Tony notices that it's raining heavy outside. He wants to know if there's a typhoon. He checks the site at the home page, scrolled down and found that there was no typhoon, only heavy for the next 3 hours.

Nick is hungry and wants to order food outside and get it delivered to their house. He wants to know the best selling food at the closest restaurant to his home. He checks the site on the "Directory" tab and found the nearest retaurants in the area. He check the menu, called through phone and ordered. Delivery arrived and Nick ate and was happy.

Kendy had a friend who was a member of the chamber and heard it's benefits. Her friend recommended her to the site to know about the specific details of the membership. She checks it on her phone, because she doesn't have a computer or laptop. She was able to navigate clearly. She found the "Join" tab and was able to read everything what the membership has to offer.

Christine has been living in Tarlac for a long time now. Now she wonders why the city is named "Tarlac". She checks the site and clicked on the "Discover" tab where the history about the town is explained and described. Now she knows why.

Ramon is new to town and wants to be familiar with the place. He wants a guide. He discovers the site and found the "Contacts" tab. He found who can help him.
