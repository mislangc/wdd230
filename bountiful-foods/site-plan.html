Bountiful Foods Site Plan

WDD230 - Carl Benedict Mislang

Purpose

Site Name

Bountiful Foods Fresh Fruits

Site Purpose

Selling of fruit drink mix to locals and tourists to the coasts of Carlsbad. Showcasing of large variety of fresh fruits. Providing weather update and info on available products.

Site Logo

Audience

Locals, Tourists, Surfers & Surfers of the internet, Farmers, and Food lovers.

Personas

Persona 1: Howard Dela Cruz is a 26-year old surfer. He surfs around the afternoon every weekends. Sometimes he forgots to bring food and drinks to bring with him. His home is a 10-min drive from the nearest coast. He prefers to quench his thirsts with juice drinks rather just water after surfing.

Persona 2: Maria Victoria a 20-year old college student who lives in near the coast of Carlsbad. Her school is also close to the coasts. In her break time, she would buy food and drinks before she studies on. She prefers juice drinks but she would not stick to just one flavor. She likes to order a drink that would depend on her fruit choices.

Scenarios

Carl's planning to go swimming at the beach today but notices the dark clouds. He checks the website if it's safe to go. The weather forecast tells that it's just scattered clouds, rain would pour but for a moment. Carl waited and happily went to the beach as he saw the sun appear again.

Martha strolls the beach everyday. She notices a crowd in front of the Bountiful foods. Curious, she walks closer to see what's happening. She saw the best selling drink on sale today, that's why there was a crowd. She waited in line and bought the drink.

Robert ran for 5 miles and was tired. He noticed he forgot his drink and was still a few miles away from home. Good thing there was a close business who sells drinks to refill his energy again.

Berta surfs the internet searching for fruit farm info. But her vision is not that good. That wasn't a problem since the website's design were very clear to see and she was able to navigate smoothly. She gained the information she was looking for.

Michael likes to try every fruit drink mix available. He was surprised when he learned that "customers can make their own fruit drink mix". He wants to check the available fruits this time that are available to mix. He found the mix button and was sent to the fresh page. He's know enjoying mixing and ordering drinks online.
