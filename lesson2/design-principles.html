Alignment

Amazon

amazon.com

Product arrangement are wonderfully aligned in Amazon. Item boxes are equal in size, proportion and pleasing to the eye of the consumer. Really makes you wanna scroll down more and see more of those satisfying aligned boxes.

White Space

Codecademy

codecademy.com

Codecademy use of whitespace and clean design are great. Helps the audience to have a good room of breathing while browsing the page and understand each topics because the designs are centralized in this whitespace.

Visual Hierarchy

Albion Online

albiononline.com

Albion Online site is a wonderful example of visual hierarchy. Each elements are grouped that are similar to one topic and separated if they have a different meaning. Site visitors will have a good comprehension of what the site is all about and what it can bring.
